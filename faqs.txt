What is your return policy?
We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unused and in original packaging. To start a return, contact support@example.com with your order number. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days.

How do I reset my password?
Go to the login page and click "Forgot password". Enter your registered email address and we will send you a reset link. The link expires after 24 hours. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, and bank transfers. All transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption. We do not store full card details on our servers.

How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 5–7 business days. Express shipping (2–3 business days) is available at checkout for an additional fee. International orders may take 10–20 business days depending on customs clearance.

How do I contact customer support?
You can reach our support team via email at support@example.com or by phone at +1-800-555-0100, Monday to Friday, 9am–6pm EST. Average email response time is under 4 hours during business hours.

Can I change or cancel my order?
Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that, the order enters processing and cannot be changed. Contact support immediately if you need to make changes.

Do you offer discounts for bulk orders?
Yes, we offer tiered discounts for orders over 50 units. Contact sales@example.com for a custom quote. Nonprofits and educational institutions may also qualify for special pricing.

Is my personal data safe?
We are GDPR and CCPA compliant. Your personal data is never sold to third parties. You can request a full export or deletion of your data at any time by emailing privacy@example.com.

How do I track my order?
Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number by email. You can use this number on our website's order tracker or directly on the carrier's site. Tracking updates may take up to 24 hours to appear.

What happens if my item arrives damaged?
If your item arrives damaged, take photos and contact support@example.com within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange a free replacement or full refund, whichever you prefer.
